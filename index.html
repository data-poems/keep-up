The Weight of Rent

A visualization of the housing crisis across 3,222 US counties.

Axes

Vertical (Y): Rent Burden (Reversed Scale)
Horizontal (X): Median Household Income (Log Scale)

Interpretation

Each dot is a county. Red glow indicates high rent burden relative to income.

Data: Census ACS 2022
